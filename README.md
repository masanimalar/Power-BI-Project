# Power-BI-Project

# Car Sales & Price Analysis using Power BI 🚗📊

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing car sales and pricing data to understand market trends, pricing factors, and customer preferences using Microsoft Power BI.

An interactive dashboard was developed to visualize key metrics such as average price, mileage impact, engine size influence, and price distribution across different categories.

---

## ❗ Problem Statement
In the automobile industry, pricing decisions depend on multiple factors such as mileage, engine size, brand, and vehicle condition. Without proper analysis, it becomes difficult to identify how these factors influence car prices.

This project aims to analyze car sales data to uncover pricing patterns and support better decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Analyze factors affecting car prices  
- Identify trends in car sales and pricing  
- Compare price distribution across brands and categories  
- Build an interactive dashboard for insights  

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- Microsoft Power BI  
- Dataset (Car Price Data)  

---

## ⚙️ Workflow
- Data Cleaning  
- Data Transformation  
- Data Modeling  
- Dashboard Creation  
- Data Analysis  
- Insight Generation  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features
- KPI Cards (Total Cars, Average Price, Highest Price, Lowest Price, Mileage, Engine Size)  
- Price distribution by brand  
- Price trend over years
- fuel typ, condition and price category distribution
- Mileage vs Price analysis 
- Condition vs Price comparison  
- Engine Size vs Price relationship  
- Interactive filters (Brand, Year, Fuel Type, Transmission, Condition)  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Cars with higher mileage tend to have lower prices, showing a negative relationship between mileage and price.  
- Vehicle condition significantly impacts pricing, where “Like New” cars are priced much higher than used cars.  
- Engine size has a positive influence on price, with higher engine capacity cars generally costing more.  
- Price trends show a steady increase over the years, indicating growth in the car market.  
- High-price category vehicles dominate the dataset, contributing to the majority of total revenue.
